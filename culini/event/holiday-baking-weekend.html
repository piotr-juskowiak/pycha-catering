Event Details

A curated dining experience that celebrates the season.

Join us for an intimate evening of food, wine, and conversation at our Seasonal Supper Club—a multi-course dinner event designed to highlight the freshest local ingredients and thoughtful cooking. Each supper is crafted around what’s in season, with a rotating menu that reflects the moment—whether it’s spring herbs and citrus, summer heirloom tomatoes, or cozy autumn squash and spice.

Hosted in a warm, welcoming setting, the evening begins with a small aperitif and bites before moving into a curated tasting menu paired with wine or seasonal beverages. Our chef will guide you through each course, sharing the story behind the ingredients and the inspiration behind the dishes. It’s not just a meal—it’s a shared experience.

Each supper includes:

  • A welcome aperitif and small bites
  • A multi-course seasonal tasting menu
  • Thoughtfully paired wines or non-alcoholic pairings
  • Guided chef storytelling throughout the evening

Whether you come solo or with friends, this is the perfect event for food lovers who appreciate intentional cooking, great conversation, and a beautifully set table. Seating is limited to keep things personal and relaxed, so be sure to reserve your place early.
