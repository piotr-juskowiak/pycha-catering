We hear from our clients

I’ve tried pizza all over the city, but Forkly’s wood-fired crust is something else. The toppings taste fresh, the cheese melts perfectly, and the smoky flavor gives it a homemade touch. Even when I order delivery, the quality stays the same, which is rare. Easily my favorite pizza spot now.

We ordered a mix of wings, fries, and pizza for a family night, and every single dish was a hit. The wings were perfectly crispy without being greasy, the fries stayed crunchy, and the pizza was still hot and full of flavor. The staff went out of their way to make sure our order was just right. You can tell they really care about their food and customers.

The first thing you notice when you walk into Forkly is the amazing smell from the grill. I had the grilled chicken with sides, and it was juicy, flavorful, and cooked to perfection. The place is small, but it feels warm and welcoming, almost like eating at a friend’s house. It’s the kind of spot you want to keep coming back to.
